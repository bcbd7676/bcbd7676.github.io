Privacy Policy Privacy Policy

Arka Seeker does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

The app operates entirely on-device. Gameplay data such as scores, progress, and settings are stored locally on the user’s device.

If the user chooses to make an optional in-app purchase to remove ads, the transaction is handled directly by the user’s connected Solana wallet. Arka Seeker does not have access to wallet private keys or personal wallet data.

Third-party services such as advertising providers may collect anonymous usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

By using Arka Seeker, you agree to this privacy policy.
