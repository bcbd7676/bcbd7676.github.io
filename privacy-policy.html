Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

ArkASeeker ("we", "our", or "us") takes your privacy seriously. This policy explains how data is handled when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Data We Collect Directly

ArkASeeker itself does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. The App operates primarily on-device. Gameplay data such as scores, progress, and settings are stored locally on your device only and are never sent to us.

2. Third-Party Services

The App integrates third-party SDKs that may independently collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies. These include:

  • Google AdMob — may collect device identifiers (including advertising IDs) and usage data to serve relevant advertisements. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Services — subject to Google's standard terms for app distribution.

We do not control the data practices of these third parties. You may limit ad personalisation through your device settings under Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation, or by purchasing the Remove Ads option within the App where available.

3. Solana Wallet & In-App Purchases

If you choose to make an optional in-app purchase to remove ads, the transaction is handled directly by your connected Solana wallet. ArkASeeker does not have access to your wallet's private keys, seed phrase, or any personal wallet data. We receive no financial information from this transaction beyond confirmation that a purchase was completed.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has been affected, please contact us and we will take prompt action.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
